Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

For a client I would advise them to read up on how important security for their product is. I would suggest they read about Cross-Site Scripting, Cross-Site request forgeries and SQL injections as these are the most common ones. 2FA (two factor authorization) is becoming a lot more common, and it works really well when trying to protect your accounts/logins. Thinking about what networks you connect to, if your passwords are strong is also very important. Sanitization of your files/data is also important as that makes it so the system will only accept the right formats you have set it to use.
